Project.

This is a simple Qt project to read NMEA GPS packets and show them on a Qt GUI, it works with COM ports eg (Bluetooth, USB and others), it works perfectly with Android Bluetooth output app (Google Play)

This project support two languages: English and Portuguese

Notice: Project is on Alpha stage, you may find bugs.

GPS Satellites button group:

  • Normal: Satellites not being used.
  • Blue: Satellites active (yellow in Graphics view).

GPS Graphics view:

  • Black: Satellites not being used.
  • Yellow: Active Satellites.
  • Blue: Selected satellites.

Notes: Center represents 90°, the middle ring approximate 45° and the outside ring 0° and the North is fixed in the top
